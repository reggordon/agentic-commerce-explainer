What is Agentic Commerce?

Agentic commerce uses autonomous agents to help users select products, negotiate prices, process payments, and fulfill orders. Each step is managed by a specialized agent, making the buying process smarter and more efficient.

  • Recommender: Suggests products based on your preferences.
  • Cart Manager: Reviews and optimizes your cart.
  • Negotiator: Finds the best price for you.
  • Payment Processor: Handles secure payment.
  • Fulfillment Bot: Manages shipping and delivery.
